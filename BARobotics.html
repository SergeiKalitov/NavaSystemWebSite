Payment terms and safeguards
  1. Late fees. Overdue balances may be subject to a late fee of 1.5% per month, equivalent to 18% annually, to the extent permitted by California law.
  2. Early payment discount. A 2/10 Net 30 discount may be offered: 2% discount if payment is received within 10 days; otherwise the full balance is due within 30 days.
  3. Purchase Order requirement. A valid Purchase Order is required before work begins for all jobs exceeding $1,000.
  4. Credit check for larger accounts. Net 30 terms for balances over $10,000 are subject to a basic credit review, including D&B, public records, and unpaid litigation or bankruptcy checks.
  5. Acceptance criteria for commissioning. Commissioning contracts must include objective acceptance criteria, such as required test cycles, error-free operation thresholds, inspection milestones, or other agreed performance tests.
